Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Summary: Split App helps users record shared expenses, members, and settlement information. We use personal information only to provide, secure, maintain, and improve the app. We do not sell personal data.

1. Scope

This Privacy Policy explains how Split App ("the App") handles information when you use the App, related web pages, and cloud services. It is prepared with reference to Japan's Act on the Protection of Personal Information and related guidance.

Please also review our Terms and Conditions.

2. Information We Collect

We collect and store information that you provide or generate when using the App, including:

  • Account information: email address, authentication user ID, display name or profile name, and profile image URL when provided or available through authentication/profile features.
  • Event and member information: event names, members, nicknames, emails added for member invitations or matching, invite codes, invitation identifiers, roles, and membership status.
  • Expense and settlement information: payment subjects, payers, participants, amounts, currencies, contribution details, calculated balances, settlement summaries, and related timestamps.
  • Friends and member links: friend/member relationships, placeholder member links, and related identifiers used to show the correct people in shared events.
  • Technical information: authentication/session data, device or app environment information, logs, error information, and information needed to protect the service from unauthorized access or abuse.

3. How We Use Information

We use information for the following purposes:

  • To create and manage accounts and authenticate users
  • To create, join, display, and manage events, members, payments, currencies, and settlement summaries
  • To generate invite links or codes and allow invited users to join shared events
  • To maintain data consistency, migrate legacy data, troubleshoot issues, and prevent misuse
  • To respond to inquiries, deletion requests, and other support requests
  • To comply with applicable laws and protect our rights, users, and services

4. Cloud Storage and Service Providers

Split App uses cloud services such as Firebase and Google Cloud to provide authentication, database, storage, server processing, and related infrastructure. Your information may be processed and stored on servers operated by these service providers.

We use service providers only as necessary to operate the App and require them to handle information appropriately according to their roles and applicable law.

5. Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We may disclose information only in the following cases:

  • Within shared events: event members may see information needed for the event, such as member names, payment details, currencies, and settlement summaries.
  • With service providers: cloud infrastructure and technical service providers process data on our behalf.
  • With your direction or consent: for example, when you share an invite code or link with another person.
  • Legal or safety reasons: when required by law, legal process, or necessary to protect users, the App, or our rights.

6. Advertising and Tracking

We do not use your personal data for third-party advertising sales. We do not state that the App includes cross-app or cross-site tracking for advertising purposes. If this changes, we will update this Privacy Policy and any required platform disclosures.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain information for as long as needed to provide the App, maintain shared event records, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and protect the service.

You may request deletion of your account or personal information by contacting us. Some records may remain where needed for shared event integrity, legal compliance, security, backups, or legitimate operational purposes.

8. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, alteration, or disclosure. However, no internet or cloud service can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

9. International Processing

Because we use cloud service providers, information may be processed or stored outside your country or region. We handle such processing in accordance with applicable privacy laws and service provider safeguards.

10. Children's Privacy

Split App is not directed to children. If you believe a child has provided personal information without appropriate consent, please contact us.

11. Your Rights and Requests

Subject to applicable law, you may request disclosure, correction, suspension of use, deletion, or other handling of your personal information. Please contact us using the email address below. We may need to verify your identity before responding.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above. For significant changes, we will provide notice through the App, this page, or another appropriate method.
