Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

Summary: Shopping List stores your grocery list data primarily on your device. If you use the app's AI-assisted sorting or parse-and-sort features, the text you submit is sent through our backend for processing.

1. Information We Collect

Shopping List processes the following information when you use the app:

  • Grocery item text you create and edit in the app
  • Pasted text you choose to send for parse-and-sort
  • Basic app state needed to save and display your list on your device
  • Limited technical data used to protect the AI service, such as a hashed IP-based rate-limit identifier on our backend

2. Local Data Storage

Your grocery list data is primarily stored locally on your device using on-device app storage. This allows you to use the core list management features without creating an account or relying on a remote database for everyday list storage.

3. AI-Assisted Features

If you use AI-assisted sorting, the text of your current grocery items is sent to our backend so the items can be categorized and reordered.

If you use parse-and-sort, the pasted text you submit is sent to our backend so it can be converted into structured grocery items and categorized.

These requests are routed through Firebase Cloud Functions and processed by OpenAI through our backend. Please avoid submitting sensitive personal information through these optional AI features.

4. No Account Required

Shopping List does not require an account to use its core features. You can create, edit, check off, reorder, and delete grocery items without signing up or providing your email address.

5. How We Use Information

We use information only to operate the app and support its features, including:

  • Saving and displaying your grocery list on your device
  • Processing AI-assisted sorting and parse-and-sort requests that you initiate
  • Protecting the backend against abuse through request validation, App Check, and rate limiting
  • Diagnosing service issues and maintaining reliability

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data.

We may share information only in the following circumstances:

  • Service providers: With providers that help operate the AI features, including Firebase Cloud Functions hosting and OpenAI processing
  • With your direction: When you choose to use AI-assisted sorting or parse-and-sort
  • Legal requirements: When disclosure is required by law or reasonably necessary to protect rights, safety, or the service

7. Data Retention and Deletion

Locally stored grocery list data remains on your device until you edit or delete it, or until you remove the app from your device.

Backend service data used for operational protection, such as rate-limit records, may be retained for a limited period according to our infrastructure and operational needs.

8. Security

We use reasonable technical measures to protect the backend services that power AI-assisted features. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Children's Privacy

Shopping List is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children through the app.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the app or our practices. Any updates will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above.
