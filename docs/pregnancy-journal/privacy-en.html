Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Summary: Pregnancy Journal is designed with your privacy in mind. All your data stays on your device. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information.

Pregnancy Journal operates entirely on your device. Your journal entries, photos, videos, and all other data are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to us or any third party.

2. Data Storage

All data you create in Pregnancy Journal is stored locally on your device:

  • Journal entries and text
  • Photos and videos you add to entries
  • Pregnancy profile information
  • App preferences and settings

This data is not uploaded to any server, cloud service, or external storage.

3. No Account Required

Pregnancy Journal does not require you to create an account, provide an email address, or sign in with any service. You can use the app immediately without providing any personal information.

4. No Analytics or Tracking

We do not use any analytics services, tracking tools, or advertising networks. We do not collect:

  • Usage statistics
  • Device information
  • Location data
  • Advertising identifiers

5. No Third-Party Services

Pregnancy Journal does not integrate with or send data to any third-party services. Your data remains completely private and under your control.

6. Photos and Camera Access

The app requests permission to access your camera and photo library solely to allow you to add photos and videos to your journal entries. These media files are:

  • Stored locally on your device
  • Never uploaded or shared
  • Accessible only within the app

7. Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • You can delete individual entries at any time within the app
  • Uninstalling the app will remove all app data from your device
  • There is no data on our servers to delete because we never receive your data

8. Data Backup

Your Pregnancy Journal data may be included in your device's backup (iCloud Backup or local backup) according to your device settings. These backups are managed by Apple and are subject to Apple's privacy policy.

9. Children's Privacy

Pregnancy Journal is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
